sandbox menu controls
to enter sandbox menu enter "sandbox" or "s"
  to reset your cookies just like in the normal version default enter "reset" or "r"
  to get nearlly unlimited cookies and multiplier enter "unlimited" or "un"
  to 2x your cookies and multiplier enter "2"
  to choose how many cookies and multiplier you have enter "choose" or "co"
  to exit sandbox menu enter "exit" or "esc"
