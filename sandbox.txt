sandbox menu controls
to enter sandbox menu enter "sandbox"
  to reset your cookies just like in the normal version default enter "reset"
  to get back your cookies and multiplier just like in the sandbox default enter "back"
  to 2x your cookies and multiplier enter "2"
  to reset your cookies to 0 enter "cookie"
  to reset your multiplier to 1 enter "multi"
  to get your cookies and multiplier to 0 enter "null"
  to exit sandbox menu enter "exit"
